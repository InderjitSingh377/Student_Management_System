............................................Student Satisfaction Survey - Teaching, Learning, and Evaluation............................................

Chandigarh University is conducting a Student Satisfaction Survey regarding Teaching – Learning and Evaluation, which will help university to improve quality of teaching, enhancing connect with students as a key stakeholder, and address important issues / deficiencies (if /any) in a timely manner.

IMPORTANT NOTE :
1) A student will have to respond to all the questions given in the given format with sincere effort and thought.
2) All questions should be compulsorily attempted, for questions having five responses, choose the most appropriate one, and for subjective questions provide the requested information.
3) Kindly restrict your response to University’s teaching learning / evaluations / academic processes only.
4) Please fill the survey latest at the earliest as per issued timeline by the Registrar office.
